SQA Advanced Higher Maths

05/06/2020

Scope

This tutorial aims to cover the material for the SQA advanced higher maths course as of the 2019-2020 session. It aims to give in depth explanations of the topics in order to improve understanding. The aim of the tutorial is not to improve the use of the techniques required in the syllabus since that requires practice of many example which the student will have to find elsewhere.

In truth this tutorial is written for me to consolidate the mathematical concepts I learnt last year but I hope it can help somebody else too. Advanced Higher Maths covers the beginning of many university topics and I hope a solid understanding can help me when I study Mathematics next academic year.

Prerequisites

The student should have an solid understanding of the ideas and techniques from Higher Maths.

Tutorials

I have split the course into 3 areas. At the moment of writing these are:

  • Calculus
  • Linear Algebra
  • Number Theory and Proof
